The setting is a grand annual community potluck in a small town, celebrated for its camaraderie and delectable dishes. This year, however, the event is marred by tragedy when several guests fall severely ill, and one, unfortunately, does not survive. The victim, a beloved local teacher known for their passion for food and community, succumbed to a severe infection. Initial investigations point to the potluck as the source of the infection. Players are called in to uncover which dish was contaminated, who brought it, and whether it was a tragic accident or a malicious act.
